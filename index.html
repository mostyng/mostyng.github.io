Mostyn Griffith is an interaction designer interested in facilitating and creating experiences that promote social equity, collaboration, and ecological awareness.

He currently works as a research-focused, visually-driven, user experience designer and digital strategist for Login.gov via the General Services Administration.

He also enjoys cooperating with four of his close friends from college. They go by the name Merl. Check out some of their work here.

He has collaborated with clients like IBM Watson, Facebook, Uber, LinkedIn, WeWork, Evan Roth, Zenni, IHS Markit, Pearson, and The People's Lobby.
