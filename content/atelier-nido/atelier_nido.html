Atelier Nido Architecture Studio Branding

Atelier Nido is an architecture studio led by Jules Romier. With over a decade of experience in luxury residential and architectural design, Romier has shaped projects ranging from Hamptons estates to Miami interiors and boutique island retreats.

Romier approached me to refresh his brand to celebrate the maturing and growth of his luxury architecture studio over 2024.

The original logo shown below featured a high contrast monogram accompanied with a humanist typeface. I looked to improve the monogram's legibility across various sizes and applications.

My process began with sketching out a wide range of monogram styles. As a luxury architecture studio, we wanted to evoke a timeless elegance.

We took inspiration from classic serif and script-based typefaces, authoratative slab serif monograms, all the way to vintage humanist san-serif typefaces

The final logo ended up being a combination of the first and second option. Romier enjoyed the uppercase version of the typeface Adelia with its unique "LI" ligature that creates a special moment of interest in the wordmark.

The monogram was constructed in reference to Adelia's tiny serifs which give it its distinct, refined character, differentiating it from other humanist serifs.

We extended the brand into multiple forms of collateral including stationery, business cards, a slide deck template, totes, and a website landing page.
