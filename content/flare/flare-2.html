Flare: The Ultimate App for Groups

Flare is the one-stop-shop for group communication, events, and management. It's a product I've worked alongside Jack Chen and Daniel Breyer since 2017.

I joined full-time as the Head of Design in August 2022. During my tenure daily active users scaled from 20K to 150K. To this day, Flare is used by over 350K people monthly in over 3,000 student groups, clubs, fraternities, and sororities at over 300 colleges all over the United States.

Our team was able to secure 3M dollars in seed funding from Floodgate, Bessemer, and Goodwater following our success in rapidly growing the user base from 2022 to 2023.
