Kembara Restaurant Branding

Kembara is a Phoenix, AZ based restaurant created by Chef Angelo Sosa and restaurateur, Mark Stone. The inspiration for this restaurant concept came from their travels throughout East and Southeast Asia. For over a month they ate their way through night markets to street-side vendors in countries like Vietnam, Malaysia, Thailand, and Taiwan.
